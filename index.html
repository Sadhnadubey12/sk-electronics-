Our Service Guidelines

These terms govern the use of SK-ELECTRO's electric fencing and LED lighting services. Please review them before proceeding with our services.

  • 01

    Service Agreement

    Clients must enter into a formal agreement before any installation or service work begins. This outlines project scope, duration, and costs.

  • 02

    Product Warranty

    All electric fencing and LED lighting components come with a limited warranty. Faulty installation or third-party tampering voids the warranty.

  • 03

    Installation Policy

    Installations are carried out by certified SK-ELECTRO technicians. Customers are responsible for ensuring safe and accessible workspaces.

  • 04

    Payments & Deposits

    A deposit is required to confirm any booking. Remaining balance is due upon project completion unless otherwise agreed in writing.

  • 05

    Cancellations

    Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. Deposits may be forfeited if cancellation occurs after materials have been prepared.

  • 06

    Liability Disclaimer

    SK-ELECTRO is not liable for any damages resulting from misuse, neglect, or third-party alterations of installed systems.
